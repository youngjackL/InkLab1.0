Our Story

More Than Ink —
It's a Legacy

Founded by Timm — a master tattoo artist with over a decade of experience — Timm Ink Lab was born from a relentless pursuit of perfection. Every piece we create is a collaboration between artist and canvas, a conversation between vision and skin.

We specialise in custom designs tailored to your story. Whether it's a whisper-fine single needle piece or a bold statement sleeve, our studio is your sanctuary.

Located in Thika's Runda estate, our sterile, welcoming environment ensures every client feels at home from the first consultation to the final reveal.

Our Specialities

Every style, every vision — executed with the precision of a master and the care of a craftsman.

Blackwork & Tribal

Bold, graphic designs with deep roots in tradition. From geometric precision to organic tribal motifs, our blackwork speaks with authority.

Fine Line

Delicate single-needle work that ages gracefully. Perfect for subtle, sophisticated statements — floral, geometric, or abstract.

Sleeve portraits

Minimalist Tattoos
